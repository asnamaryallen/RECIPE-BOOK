Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette
  • 3 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Fresh basil, 2 tbsp olive oil, salt
  • Balsamic vinegar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast or grill baguette slices.
  2. Mix tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, and season.
  3. Spoon mixture onto bread. Drizzle balsamic vinegar if desired.
  4. Serve immediately.
