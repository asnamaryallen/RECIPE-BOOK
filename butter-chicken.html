Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (boneless)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala, salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt for at least 1 hour.
  2. Grill or pan-fry chicken until cooked and slightly charred.
  3. Sauté onions in butter until golden, add ginger-garlic paste, then tomatoes and spices. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Optional: Blend sauce for smoothness. Add cream and chicken. Simmer for 10–15 mins.
  5. Garnish and serve hot with naan or rice.
