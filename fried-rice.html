Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (cold)
  • 1 cup chopped vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic, chopped
  • Spring onion, salt, oil

Instructions

  1. Saut√© garlic in hot oil, add veggies, stir-fry on high flame.
  2. Add cold rice, mix gently.
  3. Season with soy sauce, salt, and toss until evenly mixed.
  4. Garnish with spring onion. Serve hot.
