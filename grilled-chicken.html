Grilled Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chili powder, salt, pepper
  • Fresh herbs (thyme, rosemary)

Instructions

  1. Mix yogurt, lemon juice, garlic paste, spices, and herbs into a marinade.
  2. Coat chicken well and refrigerate for 2+ hours.
  3. Grill or oven-bake at 200Â°C until browned and cooked inside.
  4. Rest before serving with chutney or veggies.
