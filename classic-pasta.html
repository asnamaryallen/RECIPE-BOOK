Classic Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil, salt, pepper, Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta until al dente. Drain and save some water.
  2. Saut√© garlic in olive oil, add tomatoes, and cook till soft.
  3. Add pasta and toss well. Use pasta water for moisture.
  4. Season and top with basil and cheese. Serve warm.
